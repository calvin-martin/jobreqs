# Marketing Requirements - Claude Template

This file contains Marketing-specific adaptations of the general requirements template.

## Marketing-Specific Categories

1. **Marketing Fundamentals**: Core marketing principles, customer psychology
2. **Digital Channels & Platforms**: Social media, email, content marketing, SEO/SEM
3. **Analytics & Measurement**: Campaign tracking, ROI analysis, data interpretation
4. **Content Creation & Strategy**: Writing, design, brand consistency, storytelling
5. **Campaign Management**: Planning, execution, optimization, A/B testing
6. **Stakeholder Communication**: Presenting results, cross-functional collaboration
7. **Strategic Marketing Leadership**: (Senior levels) Team management, strategy development

## Marketing Level Progression

- **Level 1**: Marketing Trainee → Junior Marketing Specialist
- **Level 2**: Junior → Marketing Specialist
- **Level 3**: Marketing Specialist → Senior Marketing Specialist
- **Level 4**: Senior → Marketing Manager/Lead
- **Level 5**: Manager → Marketing Director

## Reference Quality Standards

For Marketing requirements, prioritize:
- **Platform-specific tutorials** (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, HubSpot, etc.)
- **Case studies** with actual campaign results and learnings
- **Current best practices** (marketing changes rapidly)
- **Tool training** with hands-on exercises
- **Industry reports** with actionable insights
